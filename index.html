Rajeev Yasarla

I am a Senior Machine Learning Researcher at Qualcomm AI Research, with a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Johns Hopkins University (advised by Dr. Vishal M. Patel). My research spans Computer Vision, Machine Learning, 3D Vision, and Robotics. I have worked on Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for Robotics and end-to-end autonomous driving, video depth estimation, image restoration/enhancement, image segmentation, and domain adaptation. I received the Best Paper Award — IEEE ICIP 2021.

I had the opportunity to intern at:

  1. AIBEE US Corp. (Amir Sadeghian) — self-supervised salient object detection.
  2. Microsoft (Hamid Reza Vaezi Joze) — Face enhancement under noise/blur/low-light; project patented: US Patent 11,526,972.
